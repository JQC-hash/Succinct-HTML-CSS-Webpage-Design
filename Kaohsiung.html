Lamb Hotpot @ Ming Der Lamb Hot Pot

Taiwanese people love hotpot all year round. When the winter begins, lamb hotpot can be found in every househould table. It is part of the traditional food remedy. It is believed to mildly supplement the body with nutrients to prepare for the coming winter. The soup is the suol of a lamb hotpot. The typical base soup consists of slow-cooked lamb bone broth and time-tested traditional herbal formula. The herbs complement the flavor of the lamb, and accentuate the natural aroma of the meat. Kanshan's farming industry provides fresh ingredients everyday, making it the perfect town to have the lamb hotpot.

Ingredients:

  • Lamb-bone and traditional herbs sachet to make broth
  • Lamb meat
  • Leafy vegetables
  • Assorted mushrooms, especially needle mushroom
  • Tofu, corn, etc.
