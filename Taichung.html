Bubble Tea @ Chun Shui Tang

A chewy drink? Not so sure? However, the perfect combination of tea, milk and chewy tapioca balls is definely worth a try. A touch of cane sugar's natural aroma embeded in the slow-cooked tapioca balls brings all the ingridients together. Have a picky tongue? Not a problem, all the drinks can be customized, sweeteners, icyness and toppings. Just tell what you fancy!


Taro Cake @ Shan Mai Pastry

Need something to go with the tea? Taro cake is the perfect match! Shan Mai's taro cakes use the best 40%-50% part of each Dajia's famous betel nut taro which is rich in vitamins, food fibers and nutrients. The taro cake has a beautiful natural shade of light purple, creamy flavor and smooth taste, living up to its name as a symbolic Taichung's specialty.
