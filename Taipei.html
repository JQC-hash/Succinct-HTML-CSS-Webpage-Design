4 Steps to Eat Soup Dumplings

  1. Break a small hole and let the soup sip out to the spoon.
  2. Taste the soup by itself.
  3. Put some baby ginger and soy sauce on the soup dumplings.
  4. Eat the whole dumpling in one go.
