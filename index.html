Taiwan is one of the safest and most friendly place to visit. Most importantly, it has highly diversified cuisines. With a broad spectrum from upper end to popular street food, lives from all walks can fulfill their love of food here. Each city has its own food sensaion. This website gives you a short introduction about Taiwan's food history and must-eats of Taipei, Taichung, Tainan and Kaohsiung.
